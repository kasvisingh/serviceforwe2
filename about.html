About

Kasvi Singh is a high school student from Design Tech High School. She excels in all her class and even involved in numerous college classes. Kasvi enjoys getting involved with her school and community. She is part of robotics, model united nations, leadership, yearbook and has started a girls who code club at her school.
Kasvi is very passionate about music as she sings with the Peninsula Girls Chorus and plays violin with Peninsula Youth Orchestra from a very young age.
Kasvi has been very involved with her community you can find her in art fairs, book fairs, beaches, a regular library volunteer, and many school events.
She created this company so that other students have the opportunity to find more community services whether its for school or just for fun.
