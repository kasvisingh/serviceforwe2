Service for We

Service for We allows students to find different community service opportunities around the Bay Area ranging from healthcare, community, education work. This is espically great for students whose school requires a minumun community service requirement. Also, great for different companies in need of help.

This is will always be updated with upcoming events. Some of the events in include beach clean up, volunteer at library, food bank, tutoring service, animals shelters, hospitals and so much more. Each student is able to find what they are passionate about to try to make a difference in their community.
